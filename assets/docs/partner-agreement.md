# Partner Agreement

This Partner Agreement governs participation in the SISO Partner Program.

## Program Overview
- Commission on qualified referrals and transactions.
- Non-exclusive, non-transferable partnership.

## Partner Obligations
- Represent SISO accurately and professionally.
- Comply with all applicable laws and brand guidelines.

## Commission & Payment
- Default rate: 25% on eligible net revenue, unless otherwise agreed.
- Paid monthly with a 30-day clearance period.

## Term & Termination
- Either party may terminate with notice.
- Outstanding eligible commissions will be paid per the schedule.

## Liability & Indemnity
- Mutual indemnities for IP infringement and gross negligence.

## Miscellaneous
- Governing law and dispute resolution as specified by SISO.
