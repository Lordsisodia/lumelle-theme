# Commission & Payment Terms

## Commission Structure
- Standard tier: 25%
- Performance tiers: up to 35% based on volume and quality.

## Eligibility
- Commissions apply to eligible net revenue after refunds, chargebacks, and taxes.

## Payouts
- Monthly payouts, 30-day clearance window.
- Payment methods: Stripe Connect, bank transfer, or supported wallets.

## Taxes
- Partners are responsible for taxes in their jurisdiction.

## Disputes
- Raise disputes within 30 days of payout.
